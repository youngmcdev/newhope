New Hope Baptist is an independent Baptist church seeking to glorify God in all we do. We approach this goal by offering discipleship to all believers, proclaiming the good news of salvation in Jesus Christ, and ministering to the needs of those around us.

Our church is a wonderful cross section of age groups from infants to senior saints. We strive to meet the spiritual needs of all ages through our ministries. Please feel free to contact us with any questions you may have concerning our ministries or your spiritual needs.

A welcome from our Pastor

Thank you for taking the time to visit! We are delighted to have you as a guest at our site. I trust that it will be helpful to you in seeking more information about the ministry the Lord has allowed us to have here.

We believe that God’s Word provides the only solid foundation on which to build our lives and that God has a purpose for every life. It is our desire to have a Christ-centered ministry, to see people come to know the Lord Jesus Christ as their personal Savior, and to assist them in becoming actively involved in a Bible-believing, Bible-preaching church that cares for their family.

If we can be of any assistance please don’t hesitate to call, you may contact me at any time. I would like to extend a personal invitation for you to “Come to New Hope and See the Difference”!

Tadd Butler
Pastor
